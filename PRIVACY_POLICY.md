# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026  
**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Elli Portfolio ("Project", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise handles your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use the contact form or interact with the project, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Message content
- Optional contact information

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the project website, we automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited
- Time and date of visits
- Referrer source
- Device information

### Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that collect information:
- **EmailJS** - For contact form processing
- **GitHub** - For hosting and analytics
- **Vercel** - For deployment and analytics
- **Framer Motion** - For animations (client-side only)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Respond to your inquiries and messages
- Improve the project and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Monitor project usage and performance
- Prevent fraudulent activity
- Comply with legal obligations

We **do not**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share information with third parties for marketing
- Use information for purposes other than stated

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all transmissions
- Secure credential management
- Regular security audits
- No sensitive data in version control
- Environment variable protection

### Data Retention

- **Contact Form Data:** Retained for 90 days, then deleted
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 12 months
- **User Communications:** Retained as long as the conversation is active
