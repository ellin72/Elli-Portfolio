# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community will have an enjoyable and fulfilling experience.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community interactions in repositories
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Comments and feedback
- Direct communications related to this project

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

### ✅ Do
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Provide constructive feedback and suggestions
- Help others learn and grow
- Give credit where credit is due

### ❌ Don't
- Use harassing, abusive, or offensive language
- Make personal attacks or insults
- Engage in trolling or intentional disruption
- Share other people's private information
- Discriminate based on protected characteristics
- Make unwelcome advances or harassment
- Engage in any form of discrimination

## Expected Behavior

All contributors are expected to:
1. **Be Respectful** - Treat everyone with respect and courtesy
2. **Be Inclusive** - Welcome people from all backgrounds
3. **Be Collaborative** - Work together constructively
4. **Be Professional** - Maintain professionalism in all interactions
5. **Be Constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and suggestions

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Threats or violent language
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking
- Doxxing (publishing private information)
- Spam or low-effort contributions
- Plagiarism or copyright infringement

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document the incident** - Record details of what happened
2. **Contact the maintainer** - Email conduct@example.com
3. **Include information:**
   - What happened
   - Who was involved
   - When it occurred
   - Any relevant links or evidence
   - How you would like it to be handled

### Confidentiality
- Reports will be handled with discretion
- Your identity will be protected
- No retaliation will be tolerated

## Enforcement

### Action Levels

**Level 1 - Warning:**
- First minor violation
- Private warning and explanation
- Clear expectation for future behavior

**Level 2 - Temporary Suspension:**
- Repeated violations
- Serious single violation
- Temporary ban from participation (1-7 days)

**Level 3 - Permanent Ban:**
- Severe or repeated violations
- Permanent removal from project
- Possible report to GitHub

## Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was unfair, you may appeal within 7 days by contacting the maintainer with:
- Your appeal statement
- Additional context or evidence
- Why you believe the action was unjust
